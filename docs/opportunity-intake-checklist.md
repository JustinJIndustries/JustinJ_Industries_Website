# Opportunity Intake Checklist

## Required Intake Fields
1. Opportunity title
2. Agency/buyer
3. Solicitation number
4. Opportunity URL
5. Deadline
6. NAICS/PSC listed in the opportunity
7. Scope summary
8. Required services
9. Required registrations/certifications
10. Accessibility requirements
11. Security/privacy requirements
12. Past performance requirements
13. Submission format
14. Questions due date
15. Owner notes
